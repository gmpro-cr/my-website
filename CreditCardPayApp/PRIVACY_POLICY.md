# Privacy Policy for Credit Card Pay

**Last updated: November 9, 2025**

## Introduction

Credit Card Pay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Username, email address, phone number
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials (encrypted)
- **Biometric Data**: Face ID/Touch ID (stored locally on your device only)

### Financial Information
- **Credit Card Details**: Card numbers (masked), bank names, due dates, balances
- **Transaction History**: Payment records, transaction IDs
- **Payment Methods**: UPI IDs and preferred payment methods

### Device Information
- Device ID
- Operating system version
- App version
- IP address
- Device type and model

### Usage Information
- App interactions and navigation patterns
- Feature usage statistics
- Crash reports and error logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Process credit card payments
   - Display account information
   - Send payment reminders

2. **Improve Our App**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features

3. **Security**
   - Prevent fraud
   - Detect unauthorized access
   - Secure your account

4. **Communication**
   - Send transaction confirmations
   - Provide customer support
   - Send important updates

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
- **End-to-End Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted
- **Secure Storage**: Passwords and tokens stored in iOS Keychain
- **SSL/TLS**: All network communications use HTTPS
- **Biometric Authentication**: Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection

### Data Retention
- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Transaction history: Retained for 7 years (regulatory requirement)
- Logs: Retained for 90 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability**: Export your data in a standard format
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics and marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@creditcardpay.app
