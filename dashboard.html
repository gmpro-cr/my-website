Help & FAQ

How to make a payment?

Click on any credit card, view the details, and click "Pay Now". Select your payment amount and UPI method to complete the payment.

What UPI methods are supported?

We support Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and direct UPI ID payments.

How do I view my payment history?

All your payment history is displayed on the dashboard below your recent transactions.

When should I pay my credit card bill?

You should pay at least the minimum due amount before the due date to avoid late fees. Paying the total due amount helps you avoid interest charges.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all your data is stored locally in your browser and is not sent to any server.
